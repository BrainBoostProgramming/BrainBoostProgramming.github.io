Technology Use Paradigm

It is important that we continually look at how we use technology and the impact it has on each of us as well as our community. Please discuss the following thoughts in a 3 or 4 brief sentences.

  1. During this course, have you maintained a recommended body position while programming?
  2. With skils you have now, you are more useful to others and you may use technology more. Is the increased use of a computer better for you as an individual?
  3. Is ​your​ increased use of a computer better for your community (think of your group of friends for this one)?
  4. Here’s an interesting question -- what would your parents/guardians feel about you increasing your amount of time using a computer? Do your parents’ beliefs influence your views on computer use?
